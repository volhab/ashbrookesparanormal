Ashbrooke Cryptid Sightings

Out of all the towns in the dense state of New Jersey, ASHBROOKE (founded in 1675) recieves the most reports of paranormal activity. Located in South Jersey just west of the Pine Barrens, Ashbrooke has become a haven for apparitions, shadow ghosts, orbs, and even CRYPTIDS. This blog is an archive of all things supernatural that the tiny town has to offer, along with a gallery full of submissions from fellow Believers in the Paranormal.
